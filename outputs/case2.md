### C3: Atorvastatin 80 mg + Amlodipine CYP3A4 interaction creates elevated myopathy risk at maximum statin dose
**Urgency:** within_1_week  
**Requires physician decision:** True

**Positions:**
- *Pharmacist Agent*: Amlodipine moderately inhibits CYP3A4, increasing atorvastatin exposure. At 80 mg (maximum dose), myopathy/rhabdomyolysis risk is elevated above baseline. Requires active surveillance.
- *Clinical Team (prescribing)*: High-intensity statin (80 mg) is guideline-recommended for secondary stroke prevention with LDL 142. Dose reduction would compromise efficacy.

**Resolution options:**
- Maintain current regimen with active myopathy surveillance — baseline CK, patient education on symptoms, recheck CK and LFTs at 4–6 weeks — *Trade-off: Preserves guideline-adherent secondary prevention while managing interaction risk through monitoring. Acceptable approach.*
- Switch to rosuvastatin (not a CYP3A4 substrate) at equivalent high-intensity dose — *Trade-off: Eliminates interaction but introduces a medication change at discharge; rosuvastatin formulary status unknown.*
